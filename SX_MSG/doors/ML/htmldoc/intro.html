 Welcome to the MessageLister !
 ------------------------------


 SX_MSG V1.3 is an XIM-Door for the usage with the 
 SystemX BBS software only 
 and cannot be used under other BBS programms!


 The MessageLister is nice to use with upcoming 
 Fido software or other  Netwares for SystemX,
 or simpley to make your Messagebases under SystemX most
 visible and easy in its handling of messages.


 This Door is a basic instrument, to give overview 
 to all the configured MessageBases.
 It shows the existing MessageBases in a conference, 
 total Msgs, new ones and a lot more...
